Our Mission

Our mission is to help bring the world into a new future, through digitalization and advanced technologies. To fulfill IoT development needs and grant access to technological expertise and innovation, Indeema provides industry-leading engineering services. We don’t just build software, firmware, and hardware. We bring the power of the connected world to businesses.

Our History

Company

  • STARTED AS AN IT EDUCATIONAL PLLUG COMMUNITY

    Students and university professors formed a community to share their technological knowledge and assist anyone interested in information technology.

  • PLLUG COMMUNITY EDUCATED OVER 500 STUDENTS

    PLLUG Community provided IT education to more than 500 students. It offered a variety of courses related to embedded engineering, mobile and web development, and QA engineering.

  • FOUNDATION OF INDEEMA SOFTWARE INC.

    In September 2014, Indeema Software Inc. was founded by three Ukrainians - Shevchyk Volodymyr Bogdanovych, Rybiy Yaroslav Petrovych and Shevchyk Volodymyr Oleksiyovych.

  • LAUNCH OF IOT & IIOT LAB

    Indeema Software Inc. has established an IoT & IIoT division to provide outstanding engineering services in the IoT domain, as well as R&D and MVP development services. This division enables the company to offer the development of innovative IoT projects to its clients.

  • CREATED OWN IOT SOLUTION FOR TRACKING AIR QUALITY - UBREEZ

    The Indeema team designed and built an IoT solution to prevent health risks caused by poor air quality. It is a combination of an air quality tracking device (UBox), a cloud server, and mobile applications.

  • JOINED LVIV IT-CLUSTER

  • EDUCATIONAL IOT CAMPAIGN WITH IVAN FRANKO NATIONAL UNIVERSITY OF LVIV

    The company constantly collaborates with local universities and communicates with students to educate them about the IT industry and help them advance in their careers.

Indeema's Culture and Values

Culture

We're building a culture where employees work on groundbreaking IoT projects and have the opportunity to make a long-term impact in the tech industry. We aspire to create a long-lasting organization and empower people to have the ability to lift themselves up and better their tech expertise.

AMBITIONS TO ACTIONS

We put the power in your hands, giving you the resources and opportunities you need to skyrocket your career.

CHALLENGES ACCEPTED

Employees are encouraged to explore the full potential of their skill sets, and they’re certain to grow through the job experience at Indeema.

UNITY

Trusting one another and relying on one another, teams work well together cross-functionally to accomplish their goals on the project.

RESPECT

We have a respectful workplace, where employees are encouraged to express opinions and ideas without interruption, disrespect, and humiliation. It is concerned not only with interpersonal relationships but also with any level of management.

WORK-LIFE HARMONY
