AI Applications for Drones

With our custom-built high-performance companion computer, we unlock AI-driven capabilities such as:

Autonomous Navigation & Obstacle Avoidance

AI-powered perception systems process LiDAR, cameras, and radar data to detect and avoid obstacles in real time.

Precision Agriculture

AI analyzes multispectral images to assess crop health, detect diseases, and optimize spraying or irrigation.

Autonomous Target Recognition (ATR) and Guidance

Essential for defense applications, AI models help identify and classify targets based on onboard sensor data.

Object Detection & Tracking

AI models recognize and track vehicles, humans, or wildlife, making drones effective for surveillance, security, and environmental monitoring.

Predictive Maintenance & Inspection

Drones with AI-powered vision systems detect structural damage, corrosion, or pipeline leaks before they become critical.
