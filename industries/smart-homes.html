Home Automation Solutions From Indeema Software

As a trusted partner for startups and enterprises, Indeema offers customized smart home solutions powered by IoT. Our home automation software reinvents and redefines the way we experience life, allowing businesses to harness the power of smart home automation software and deliver enhanced convenience.

FACIAL RECOGNITION AND IMAGE PROCESSING

These smart home solutions strengthen the monitoring, control and security of smart home appliances using advanced pattern recognition technologies. Indeema offers a home automation security system that deploys the Internet of Things to recognize human faces and restrict unauthorized use of smart home appliances.

PROGRAMMING CONTROLLERS, SWITCHES AND SMART HUBS

The Indeema software for home automation connects gadgets, switches and smart home appliances to a central hub. This enables businesses to control connected devices in convenient and secure ways. Smart homes solutions by Indeema make your home or office premises safer while leveraging Alexa, Apple Homekit and Google Assistant to give you total control.

MOBILE APP CONTROL AND CONFIGURATION

Businesses can control their devices in real-time using the mobile application that is a component of the smart home software solutions created by Indeema. We build end-user applications enabling enterprises to regulate, monitor and access their smart gadgets through a common mobile user interface.

Indeema also develops bespoke mobile software for enterprises enabling them to host all functions under one unified interface while managing a wide array of smart home appliances. This makes Indeema smart home automation software responsive to voice commands and other IoT core functions.

IOT SOLUTIONS FOR HVAC

Turn to Indeema smart homes solutions that work in sync with other third-party applications to deliver a seamless user experience. Enterprises can leverage IoT solutions to transform their analog HVAC systems into smart energy management and climate control solutions.

Indeema software relies on DDL units, back-end solutions and smart applications for managing HVAC systems. Our automation system stores, analyzes and processes streaming data from different devices through IoT and cloud-based services. It enhances HVAC functions through wireless sensors that transmit data to the cloud and endpoint devices.
